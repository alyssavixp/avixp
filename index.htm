Why Windows XP?

I created this website inspired by my nostalgia for my own coming of age in the early 2000s and what my family's desktop looked like at home. Since it was a shared device in the living room, and we never changed the wallpaper and we always kept everything on default settings. One of first things I can remember doing on a computer was nesting a ton of folders within each other to keep the desktop neat and make sure Bliss was as visible as possible.

In a way, I saw computer hardware as being the same as any canvas, and it's software an artist's expression come to life created by the generations of brilliant programmers and engineers who came before me.

I spent hours on that computer teaching myself HTML and CSS to be able to customize my blogs and my MySpace page. I taught myself by inspecting other people's MySpace profiles and stealing their code (sorry y'all!) and messing around with the stylization, making myself familiar with the structure of markup languages. I remember daydreaming of the day I got my first computer so I could finally change the background to be something of my own.

Twenty seven years later, I still keep Bliss as my background on all my new devices. It brings me back to a time when I was inspired by all the endless possibilities that opened up to me from that very large and very beige computer. I am still inspired by all that the computer (& the internet!) brings me.

I'm incredibly lucky to be studying and persuing a career in something I have dreamed about since childhood.
